Who We Are

Flick Orbit is a streaming platform dedicated to movie enthusiasts. Founded in 2024, our mission is to provide a seamless viewing experience with a focus on quality content and user satisfaction.

Our platform offers a diverse selection of films, from contemporary releases to timeless classics, all presented with high-quality streaming technology.

Our Technology

Flick Orbit utilizes adaptive streaming technology that optimizes video quality based on available bandwidth. Our technical infrastructure is designed to deliver consistent performance across various devices and connection speeds.
