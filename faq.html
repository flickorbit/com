Frequently Asked Questions

Is Flick Orbit really free to use?

Yes! Flick Orbit is completely free to use. We're supported by advertisements, which allow us to provide our service without charging users.

Do I need to create an account?

No, you can watch most content without an account. However, creating a free account lets you save your watch history, create favorites lists, and get personalized recommendations.

Why do I see advertisements?

Ads allow us to keep Flick Orbit free for everyone. The revenue from advertisements helps us license content and maintain our service.

What devices can I use to watch?

You can watch on any modern web browser on computers, smartphones, and tablets. We're working on apps for mobile and TV platforms.

How often is new content added?

We add new movies and shows weekly. Follow us on social media to get updates about new additions.

Can I download movies to watch offline?

Currently, we don't offer offline downloads. All content must be streamed through our platform.

Why can't I find a specific movie?

Our content library changes based on licensing agreements. Some titles may become unavailable temporarily. You can request content through our contact page.

How do I report a problem with playback?

Use the "Report Issue" button on the player or contact our support team through the contact page with details about the problem.
