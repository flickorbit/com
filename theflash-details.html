Synopsis

​The Flash is a 2023 superhero film directed by Andy Muschietti. The story follows Barry Allen, portrayed by Ezra Miller, who uses his super speed to travel back in time to prevent his mother's death. This action inadvertently alters the future, trapping Barry in a reality where General Zod has returned, threatening annihilation. With no superheroes to turn to, Barry seeks out a retired Batman and encounters Supergirl to help restore the timeline.
